Rebekah Stewartson is a student, writer, and Journalist living in New York City. She attends Euegene Lang College. Her hobbies include writing, reading, online shopping, cooking, and binge-watching Netflix TV shows.

Early Life

Birth

Rebekah Stewartson was born at Beth Isreal Hospital in Boston, Massachsuetts. She grew up in Dorchester, Massachusets with her Mother, Father, and Brother.

Education

  • Stewartson attended Inly School from Grades 3-8
  • Attended Lexingtion Christian Academy from frades 9-12
  • She is currently an undergraduate student at Euegene Lang College.
Euegene Lang College is a college under The New School University, and her expected graduation is May 2022. From the time she was young, Stewartson always had a love for music and writing, which is what influenced her decision to major in Journalism + Design. She plans to write about arts and culture after she graduates.

The New School Free Press

Picture of Februrary issue of NSFP newspaper featuring Meet Froglady

This spring semetser Rebekah joined The New School Free Press," which is her school's student-run newspaper. Rebekah has two works published on the site, one is a profile about a musican at Parsons School for Design, and the other is a photo article depicting fashion on The New School campus.
